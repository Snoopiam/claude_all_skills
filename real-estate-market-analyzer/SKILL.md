A specialized skill for comprehensive real estate market analysis, trend identification, investment evaluation, and professional reporting. Designed for real estate agents, investors, analysts, and property developers.

## Purpose

This skill assists with:
- Market trend analysis and forecasting
- Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) generation
- Investment opportunity identification
- Neighborhood and submarket evaluation
- Price trend visualization and interpretation
- Market timing recommendations
- Professional market report creation

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Market Trend Analysis
Identify and analyze patterns in:
- **Price Trends**: Appreciation, depreciation, stability
- **Inventory Levels**: Supply constraints, buyer's/seller's market
- **Days on Market**: Market temperature, demand indicators
- **Price Reductions**: Market softening signals
- **Sale-to-List Ratio**: Negotiation leverage indicators
- **Absorption Rates**: How quickly inventory sells
- **Seasonal Patterns**: Best times to buy/sell

### 2. Comparative Market Analysis (CMA)
Professional CMA generation:
- **Subject Property Analysis**: Deep dive on target property
- **Comparable Selection**: Find 3-10 similar properties (sold, active, pending)
- **Adjustment Calculations**: Square footage, condition, features, location
- **Value Range Estimation**: Low, mid, high value estimates
- **Pricing Recommendations**: List price or offer price guidance
- **Market Position**: How property compares to competition

### 3. Investment Analysis
Evaluate properties for investment potential:
- **Cash Flow Projections**: Rental income vs. expenses
- **Cap Rate Calculations**: Net operating income / purchase price
- **Cash-on-Cash Return**: Annual return on invested cash
- **Appreciation Potential**: Market trends, development plans
- **Risk Assessment**: Market volatility, tenant demand, location
- **Exit Strategies**: Hold period, appreciation targets, flip potential

### 4. Neighborhood Analysis
Evaluate location quality and trajectory:
- **Demographics**: Population trends, income levels, age distribution
- **Amenities**: Schools, shopping, parks, transit
- **Development**: New construction, infrastructure projects
- **Crime Statistics**: Safety trends
- **School Ratings**: Impact on property values
- **Walkability & Transit**: Urban appeal factors
- **Future Growth**: Zoning, master plans, economic indicators

### 5. Market Segmentation
Analyze specific property types:
- **Single-Family Homes**: Suburban market dynamics
- **Condos/Townhomes**: Urban/attached housing trends
- **Multi-Family**: Rental market, cap rates
- **Luxury Market**: High-end trends, longer cycles
- **First-Time Buyer Segment**: Entry-level inventory, affordability
- **Commercial**: Retail, office, industrial trends

## Analysis Frameworks

### SWOT Analysis Template
For any property or market:
- **Strengths**: Positive attributes, competitive advantages
- **Weaknesses**: Drawbacks, limitations, concerns
- **Opportunities**: Growth potential, emerging trends
- **Threats**: Risks, market headwinds, competition

### Porter's Five Forces (Market Competition)
- **Buyer Power**: How much leverage do buyers have?
- **Seller Power**: Is it a seller's market?
- **Competitive Rivalry**: How many similar properties?
- **Threat of Substitutes**: Alternative neighborhoods, property types
- **Barriers to Entry**: Affordability, inventory constraints

### Absorption Rate Calculation
```
Absorption Rate = Properties Sold / Months
Months of Supply = Active Listings / Absorption Rate

Example:
- 45 properties sold in last 3 months = 15/month absorption
- 30 current listings
- Months of Supply = 30 / 15 = 2 months (seller's market)

Interpretation:
- <3 months: Seller's market
- 3-6 months: Balanced market
- >6 months: Buyer's market
```

### Cap Rate Formula
```
Cap Rate = Net Operating Income (NOI) / Purchase Price

NOI = Annual Rent - Operating Expenses
Operating Expenses = Taxes + Insurance + Maintenance + Management + Vacancy

Example:
- Annual Rent: $30,000
- Operating Expenses: $9,000
- NOI: $21,000
- Purchase Price: $400,000
- Cap Rate: $21,000 / $400,000 = 5.25%
```

## Market Indicators Quick Reference

### Seller's Market Signals
- Months of supply: <3 months
- DOM: Declining or <30 days
- Sale-to-list ratio: >100%
- Price reductions: <20% of listings
- Multiple offers common

### Buyer's Market Signals
- Months of supply: >6 months
- DOM: Increasing or >60 days
- Sale-to-list ratio: <98%
- Price reductions: >40% of listings
- Negotiation leverage high

### Balanced Market
- Months of supply: 3-6 months
- DOM: 30-60 days
- Sale-to-list ratio: 98-100%
- Price reductions: 20-40% of listings
- Fair negotiations
